Rules

1.       Picks are made using point spreads provided by CBS, which they lock-in before the first game of the week.

2.       Picks are due and locked-in 5 minutes before the first game of the DAY.

3.       In order to save your picks, you have to enter picks for the entire week at one time. Then picks can be changed up until 5 minutes before the first game of each day.

4.       Tie breaker is the prediction of the score of the Monday night game. If more than one Monday night game, the tie breaker game will be shown on the pick screen.
